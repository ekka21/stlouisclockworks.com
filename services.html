Services

We specialize in Grandfather/Grandmother in-home clock servicing.

Our other services include:

  • Authorized Factory Service Center for Howard Miller, Ridgeway, Sligh, Ethan Allen, Seth Thomas, Hermle/Franz Hermle & Sohne , Electric Time Co., Inc. clocks and Chime Master Systems cast bells, bell ringing equipment & Electronic Carillon systems.
  • Repair/rebuild/restoration for mantle, wall and floor clocks.
  • In-home servicing for most ANY type of clock.
  • New clock set-up service.
  • Pick-up, delivery & set-up available at nominal cost for repairs to delicate, large & expensive clocks of all types.
  • Grandfather/Grandmother clocks prepared/packed for moving.
  • Set-up and service for relocated clocks.
  • Our Commercial Division, St. Louis Time & Signal, offers sales, fabrication, installation and service on large, commercial, civic & liturgical clocks, bells and electronic carillons.
